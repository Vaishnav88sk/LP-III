//2. Create your own wallet using Metamask for crypto transactions.[Steps]

Step 1:
Install MetaMask
Go to https://metamask.io
Click Download → Add to Chrome / Edge / Firefox
Open the extension → click Get Started
_______________________________________________

Step 2:
Create a New Wallet
Click Create a Wallet
(If you already have one, choose “Import Wallet” to restore it)
Choose whether to share feedback → click Agree or No Thanks
Set a strong password (e.g., Neha@1234)
Click Create to continue
__________________________________________________

Step 3:
Save Your Secret Recovery Phrase
Click the grey box to reveal your 12-word seed phrase
Write it down safely or store it offline (never share it)
Click each word in correct order to confirm
Click Confirm → All Done ✅
Your wallet is now ready for transactions
________________________________________________

Step 4:
Deposit or Buy Cryptocurrency
Click your wallet address to copy it (e.g., 0xAb12...F4c8)
Option 1: Send ETH from another wallet or exchange
Option 2: Click Buy to purchase ETH within MetaMask
Wait a few minutes — ETH will appear under your Assets tab
To view custom tokens, click Add Token → Enter Token Details

______________________________________________________

Step 5️:
Send Cryptocurrency
Click Send
Enter:
Recipient Address (who you’re sending to)
Amount of ETH (e.g., 0.05)
Gas Fee (higher = faster; default is auto-set)
Click Next → Confirm
Track transaction status in Activity tab or on Etherscan

____________________________________________________________

Step 6️:
Spend Crypto in a DApp
Visit a DApp from https://dappradar.com
 (e.g., KyberSwap)
Click Connect Wallet → MetaMask
MetaMask popup → Next → Confirm
Enter token swap details → click Swap Now → Confirm
Transaction is processed by miners
You can close the window and later check Activity / Etherscan

*******************************************************
Example:
Example Transaction
Amount Sent: 0.05 ETH
Gas Used: 21,000
Gas Price: 20 Gwei
Transaction Cost: 21,000 × 20 Gwei = 0.00042 ETH
